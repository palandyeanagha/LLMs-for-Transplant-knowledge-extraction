About the journal
The Turkish Journal of Pediatrics is a multidisciplinary, peer
reviewed, open access journal that seeks to publish research to
advance the field of Pediatrics. The Journal publishes original
articles, case reports, review of the literature, short
communications, clinicopathological exercises and letter to the
editor in the field of pediatrics.
