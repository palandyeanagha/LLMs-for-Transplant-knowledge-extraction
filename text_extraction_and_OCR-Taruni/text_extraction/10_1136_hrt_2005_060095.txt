Left atrial myxoma in transplanted heart
1. W W Yap,
2. K Bhattacharya,
3. V Pathi
1. 
Department of Cardiothoracic Surgery, Western Infirmary,
Glasgow, UK
1. Correspondence to:
Dr Wan W Yap
Department of Cardiothoracic Surgery, Western Infirmary,
Dumbarton Road, Glasgow G11 6 NT, UK;
wanwandoctors.net.uk
Abstract


Primary cardiac tumours are discovered in about 1 in 2000
necropsies. Cardiac myxomas make up half of all benign
intracardiac tumours. Seventy five per cent are located in the left
atrium. The first left atrial myxoma arising from the left atrium of a
transplanted heart is reported.
