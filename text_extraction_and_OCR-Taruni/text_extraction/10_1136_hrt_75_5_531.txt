Fatal giant cell myocarditis after resection of thymoma.
1. P. E. Glennon,
2. M. E. Petersen,
3. M. N. Sheppard
1. 
Cardiac Department, Royal Brompton Hospital, London.
Abstract
A case of fulminant, fatal myocarditis occurring 10 days after
resection of a benign medullary thymoma is described. A rare
association between thymoma and giant cell myocarditis is
recognised, but fulminant presentation so soon after removal of
thymoma has not previously been reported.


https://doi.org/10.1136/hrt.75.5.531
